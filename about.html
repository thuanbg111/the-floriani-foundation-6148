AboutThe Floriani Foundation

The Floriani Foundation is a U.S. 501(c)(3) public charity (EIN 861826148) formed to bring practical support to people in transition. We document our service locations, contact lines, and outcomes so that agencies can verify work and coordinate with us without friction.
